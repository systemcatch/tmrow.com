Our mission:

We want to propel humanity to a sustainable state of existence by quantifying, and making widely accessible, the impact of every choice we make

Follow @tmrowco

Natural resources are limited, and we are depleting them faster than they replenish. Our current mode of operation is unsustainable.

A radical change is required.

Information precedes action. In the jungle of complex trade-offs, doing what is right has become difficult.

We use science, data and machine learning to automatically quantify the environmental impact of your daily decisions. We present this information in a simple and elegant way, empowering everyone to take informed decisions and have a tangible and positive impact.
